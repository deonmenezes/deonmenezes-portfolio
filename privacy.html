Deon Tech Instagram Automation

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This policy explains how Deon Menezes (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when the Deon Tech Instagram Automation service interacts with the Instagram account connected by its owner.

Information we process

The service may process information made available through Meta’s Instagram Graph API, including:

  • Instagram media identifiers and comments on connected professional-account posts;
  • comment text, commenter usernames or identifiers, and timestamps;
  • message recipient identifiers and delivery status; and
  • limited operational records needed to prevent duplicate replies and diagnose errors.

How we use information

We use this information only to identify requested keywords, send the requested resource by Instagram message, prevent duplicate messages, maintain security, and troubleshoot the service. We do not sell personal information or use it for third-party advertising.

How information is shared

Information is exchanged with Meta as necessary to operate Instagram messaging and comment features. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect users, or to secure the service. We do not otherwise disclose it to unrelated third parties.

Retention and security

Operational records are retained only for as long as needed to run, secure, and troubleshoot the automation, then deleted or anonymized. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices and deletion

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete information associated with your Instagram interaction. See our data deletion instructions for the request process.

Children

The service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service or legal requirements change. The date above identifies the latest revision.
