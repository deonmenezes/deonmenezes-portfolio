Data Deletion

Last updated: July 21, 2026

You may request deletion of information processed by Deon Tech Instagram Automation in connection with your Instagram interaction.

How to request deletion

  1. Email deonmenezescodes@gmail.com with the subject “Instagram data deletion request.”
  2. Include your Instagram username and a link to, or description of, the post where you interacted.
  3. We may ask for limited additional information to verify that the request relates to you.

Do not send your Instagram password, access token, or government identification. We will never ask for your password.

What happens next

After verification, we will delete or anonymize the related comment-processing and message-delivery records that we control, unless we must retain specific information for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. We will confirm completion by email, generally within 30 days.

Disconnecting Instagram access

The connected Instagram account owner can also revoke the app’s access through Meta’s account or business integration settings. Revoking access stops future processing but does not replace a deletion request for records already held by us.

Questions

For questions about this process, email deonmenezescodes@gmail.com. See the privacy policy for more information.
