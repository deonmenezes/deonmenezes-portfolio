First, the honest bit

They aren't secret commands. They're better instructions.

ChatGPT does not have a universal hidden slash-command language. These “codes” are memorable prompt starters you can paste into a chat and customize. Better input does not guarantee a perfect answer, but it makes useful output far more likely.

01 · The foundation

The six-part prompt formula

You rarely need all six parts. Add the ones that remove meaningful ambiguity.

01Role
02Goal
03Context
04Constraints
05Format
06Quality check

Role Act as a product strategist. Goal Create a launch plan for my budgeting app. Context It helps freelancers manage irregular income. Constraints Two-person team, four weeks, $2,000 budget. Format Weekly table with owner and metric. Quality check Flag assumptions and the three biggest risks.

Give it a finish line

Define the audience, desired action, format, and what “good” means.

▤

Bring source material

Examples, notes, data, and reference text beat vague requests every time.

✦

Ask for alternatives

Request contrasting options, then choose and refine instead of accepting draft one.

✓

Verify what matters

Check important facts, calculations, citations, and decisions against primary sources.

Use AI with judgment

A quick pre-send checklist

  • ✓ Remove private, confidential, or regulated information.
  • ✓ Say when current web research and citations are required.
  • ✓ Verify high-stakes medical, legal, financial, and safety advice with a qualified professional.
  • ✓ Review the final work in your own voice before publishing or sending it.
